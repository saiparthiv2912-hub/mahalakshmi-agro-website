Our story

A family mill, run the same way for three generations.

Mahalaxmi Agro Industries started as a single huller unit beside the Cauvery delta paddy fields. Today we run a modern mill with the same relationships we started with — most of our paddy still comes from the same farming families we've bought from for decades.

We supply raw rice, parboiled (boiled) rice, and by-products like broken rice, bran and husk to wholesalers, kirana stores, hotels and hostels in bulk quantities, with consistent grading batch to batch.

How it's milled

Paddy in, packed rice out — six stages, tracked at every step.

01

Paddy intake

Farm-fresh paddy weighed and moisture-tested on arrival.

02

Cleaning

Stones, husk dust and foreign matter removed.

03

Parboiling

Optional steam treatment for boiled rice varieties.

04

Hulling

Husk removed to reveal the brown rice grain.

05

Polishing

Bran layer polished off for white, market-ready rice.

06

Grading & packing

Sorted by size, weighed and sealed for dispatch.
